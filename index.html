About

Hédi Besenyei

Hi my name is Hédi and I making jewelleries from beads. I using different techniques like peyote, soutache, embroidery, beading etc., and using different tools like soutache cord, wire, line etc. I started learning to beading about 5 years ago. First, I learnt by myself from books and the internet, then 4 years ago I found some very cool trainings. I learning to beading from Zsuzsi Veres aka. VeZsuzsi and learning to soutache from Edit Peng. My products what you can see in this site I was made by their patterns. I still learning from them in those trainings every month and I hope someday I make my own patterns.

I hope you enjoy my work.
